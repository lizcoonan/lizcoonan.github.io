Liz Coonan is a fine artist living in Queens, NY. She works primarily in textiles, painting and silkscreen. She studied Printmaking at SUNY Purchase to receive a BFA. Her most recent artwork uses the method of Batik to create designs on fabric. She also has a career as a decorative painter, making wallpapers and working on site at high end retail stores and luxury apartments in NYC.
